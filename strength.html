🏋️ Strength Dashboard Guide

This page tracks your main lifts: Bench Press, Squat, and Deadlift. You can save your numbers over time and see your progress grow.

📊 How to Use

  • Enter your current 1RM (max lift) for each movement
  • Click Save to store your results
  • Your history will appear in the chart automatically
  • Use it at the start and end of your 7-week program

🔥 What to Look For

  • Steady increases in strength over time
  • PR (personal record) jumps
  • Consistency week to week

💡 Pro Tip

Test your lifts under similar conditions each time for accurate tracking. Same warm-up, same equipment, same rest.
